Welcome to Neon Slicer

Neon Slicer turns your Apple Watch into a glowing arena of fast, addictive arcade action. Carve out territory across a neon grid while dodging deadly orbs that bounce relentlessly around the field. Claim enough of the arena to clear each level, then push into faster, tougher rounds.

How to play

  • Tap to send your slicer carving across the grid.
  • Seal off sections of the arena to claim territory.
  • Reach the target percentage to clear the level.
  • Avoid the deadly orbs — never let them hit your trail.

Features

  • One-tap arcade gameplay built for the wrist.
  • Escalating difficulty with more orbs and bigger targets.
  • Earn coins and spend them on shields, hearts, and second chances.
  • No accounts, no ads, no tracking — just play.

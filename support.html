Frequently asked

How do I clear a level?

Claim enough of the neon arena to reach the level's target percentage while avoiding the orbs.

What are coins for?

Earn coins by clearing levels and hitting milestones. Spend them on shields, extra hearts, and second chances.

Is my data safe?

Yes. Neon Slicer collects no personal data — everything stays on your Apple Watch. See the Privacy Policy.

How do I reset my progress?

Deleting and reinstalling the app removes all locally stored progress.

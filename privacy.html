Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 24, 2026

Your privacy is fully protected.

Neon Slicer does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. There are no accounts, no analytics, no advertising, no trackers, and no network connections of any kind.

The only data saved is your game progress — your best score, your token balance, and which skins and backgrounds you own and have selected. It is stored locally on your Apple Watch using the device's standard on-device storage, never leaves your device, and is removed if you delete the app.

Because no data is gathered, none can be shared, sold, or exposed.

This game is suitable for all ages.

Questions?

If you have any questions about this policy, please reach out on our Support page.

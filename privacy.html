Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 3, 2026

Your privacy matters to us.

Neon Slicer comes as two separate apps: a paid Apple Watch app, which is a one-time purchase and contains no ads, and a free iPhone & iPad app that is supported by ads (which can be removed with a one-time in-app purchase). The Advertising and Purchases sections below apply only to the iPhone & iPad app.

Neon Slicer itself does not ask for or store any personal information — there are no accounts and no sign-in.

Your game progress — your best score, your token balance, and which skins and backgrounds you own and have selected — is stored locally on your device using the device's standard on-device storage, never leaves your device, and is removed if you delete the app.

Advertising

This app shows ads through Google AdMob. To serve ads, AdMob may collect and process certain data, such as an advertising identifier, approximate location, device information, and interaction with ads, in order to display and measure advertising. You can review Google's practices at policies.google.com/technology/ads.

When you first launch the app, iOS may ask whether the app can track you. If you decline, you will still see ads, but they will be non-personalized. We do not sell your personal information.

Purchases

This app offers a one-time Remove Ads purchase. Payments are handled entirely by Apple through the App Store — we never see or store your payment card or billing details. Apple provides us only with confirmation that the purchase was made, so the app can turn off ads and restore it on devices signed in to the same Apple ID. If you buy Remove Ads, the app stops requesting ads and the advertising data described above is no longer collected.

This game is intended for a general audience.

Questions?

If you have any questions about this policy, please reach out on our Support page.
